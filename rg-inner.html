How it Works?

Our product is perfect for those in receipt of Universal Credit or Benefits who are unable to provide a suitable UK guarantor to satisfy the needs of a tenancy.

Applying For Our Service

We have streamlined our process to gather your information via Referencing & Open Banking. If we are unable to validate your information using automated services you may need to manually provide the information below.

Proof of ID

Acceptable ID includes: Passport, Drivers Licence or Citizens Card. The ID must be in date to be accepted

Apply Now

Proof of Universal Credit

Last 3 months FULL Universal Credit Statements which shows the breakdown of each of the UC calculations and your name on the UC statement

Apply Now

Proof of Benefits

Last 3 months bank statements showing the benefits being paid to you, please note we do not accept screenshots from mobile banking apps

At RentGuarantor we provide a professional guarantor service to support tenants in securing a rental property. However, if you are experiencing financial difficulties, we strongly encourage you to seek independent financial advice before applying for our guarantor service.

Support is available from: Citizens Advice, StepChange Debt Charity, National Debtline
